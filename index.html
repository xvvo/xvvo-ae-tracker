TL;DR

Lumina is an After Effects panel that automatically tracks how long you spend in each project. Open a project and it starts. Go idle or switch away and it pauses. Every session is timestamped, and you can export everything to a CSV for invoicing or get a full project stats breakdown. No internet. No account. Everything stays on your machine.

◆ How it works

Automatic, always on

Lumina polls AE every second from inside its own engine — not just the panel. Tracking keeps going even if the panel is closed.

01

Open a saved project

Lumina detects the file path instantly. Status dot turns green, timer starts counting.

02

Work normally

Timer runs in the background. AE is checked every 30 seconds for idle detection.

03

Switch apps

Grace Period gives you 60 seconds before pausing. Closing the panel doesn't stop anything.

04

Come back to AE

Timer resumes the moment AE is focused again. No lost time.

05

Saved every 10 seconds

Data is flushed to disk constantly. Even if AE crashes, you lose at most 10 seconds.

◆ FAQ

Common questions

The timer isn't starting when I open a project.
Lumina only detects saved .aep files. Unsaved new projects have no file path. Save your project first and tracking starts immediately.
It says “Paused” even though I'm actively working.
Another window is likely on top of AE. Click inside AE to bring it forward — tracking resumes instantly with Grace Period on.
Idle Pause keeps triggering when I'm active.
Idle detection watches for state changes in AE. Reviewing your comp without making changes can look like idle to Lumina. Toggle it off to disable.
Does closing the panel stop tracking?
No. Tracking runs inside AE's own scripting engine. Close the panel, undock it, hide it — the timer keeps running.
Will I lose data if After Effects crashes?
At most 10 seconds. Lumina flushes to disk every 10 seconds — crashes only cost you the current unsaved window.
Does this work on Mac and Windows?
Yes. Lumina is a standard CEP panel and handles file paths correctly on both platforms. Targets After Effects CC 2019 and all versions after.
