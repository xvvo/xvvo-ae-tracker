TL;DR

Lumina is an After Effects panel that automatically tracks how long you spend on each project. Open a saved project and it starts. Go idle or switch away and it pauses. Every session is timestamped — export everything to a CSV for invoicing or get a full project stats breakdown. One-time license activation. No subscription. Everything stays on your machine.

◆ How it works

Automatic, always on

Lumina runs inside AE's own scripting engine — not just the panel. Tracking keeps going even if the panel is closed or undocked.

01

Open a saved project

Lumina detects the file path instantly. Status dot turns green and the timer starts.

02

Work normally

Timer runs silently in the background. AE is polled every second — idle triggers after 5 minutes of no change.

03

Switch apps

Grace Period gives you 60 seconds before anything pauses. Closing the panel doesn't stop anything.

04

Come back to AE

Timer resumes the moment AE is focused again. No lost time, no manual restart.

05

Saved every 10 seconds

Data flushes to disk constantly. Even if AE crashes, you lose at most 10 seconds.

◆ Status dot

What the dot means

The small dot in the top-left of the panel tells you exactly what's happening at a glance.

Tracking
AE is focused, a project is open, timer is running.
Idle — paused
No AE activity for 5 minutes. Move anything to resume automatically.
Grace period…
AE lost focus. Timer still running — stops after 60 seconds if you don't return.
Paused / No project
No project open, or an unsaved project. Save your .aep and tracking starts immediately.

◆ FAQ

Common questions

The timer isn't starting when I open a project.
Lumina only detects saved .aep files. Unsaved new projects have no file path. Save your project first and tracking starts immediately.
It says "Paused" even though I'm actively working.
Another window is likely sitting on top of AE. Click inside AE to bring it forward — tracking resumes instantly with Grace Period on.
Idle Pause keeps triggering when I'm active.
Idle detection watches for state changes inside AE. Reviewing your comp without making any changes can look like idle to Lumina. Toggle Idle Pause off if it's bothering you.
Does closing the panel stop tracking?
No. Tracking runs inside AE's own scripting engine. Close the panel, undock it, hide it — the timer keeps running.
Will I lose data if After Effects crashes?
At most 10 seconds. Lumina flushes to disk every 10 seconds — a crash only costs you the current unsaved window.
Does this work on Mac and Windows?
Yes. Lumina is a standard CEP panel and handles file paths correctly on both platforms. Supports After Effects CC 2020 and all versions after.
